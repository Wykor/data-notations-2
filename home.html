Adobe Photoshop is a powerful image editing software widely used by professionals in graphic design, photography, and digital art. It allows users to manipulate, enhance, and create images with a variety of tools such as layers, filters, and brushes. Photoshop supports a wide range of file formats, making it versatile for both print and digital media projects. Users can perform tasks such as retouching photos, creating digital illustrations, and designing websites or advertisements. The software also includes advanced features like 3D modeling, video editing, and animation. With its customizable workspace, users can tailor the interface to suit their workflow. Photoshop's integration with other Adobe Creative Cloud apps, like Illustrator and Lightroom, enhances its functionality and streamlines the creative process. Regular updates ensure that Photoshop remains at the forefront of the digital design industry, with new tools and improvements being added frequently.
